Installation Guide

1

Download the Extension

Clone or download the repository from GitHub.

git clone https://github.com/gunaasin/ai-assistant.git
2

Open Chrome Extensions Page

Navigate to chrome://extensions/ in your Chrome browser.

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top-right corner.

4

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder you downloaded.

5

Start Using Gemini AI

The extension icon will appear in your toolbar. Click it to start using Gemini AI Assistant!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini AI Assistant free to use?

Yes, Gemini AI Assistant is completely free and open-source. You can use all features without any limitations.

Does it work on other browsers?

Currently, Gemini AI Assistant is only available for Chrome. Support for Firefox, Edge, and other browsers is coming soon.

Do I need a Google account to use it?

No, you don't need a Google account to use the basic features. However, some advanced features may require you to sign in with your Google account.

Is my data secure?

Your privacy is our top priority. All processing happens locally on your device whenever possible, and we don't store any of your personal data.
